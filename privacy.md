Last updated: March 2026

Shopya ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Shopya mobile application. This page informs users regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information.

By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

### 1. Personal Information
When you create an account or list a shop on Shopya, we may collect:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Shop details (name, category, location)

### 2. Device Information
We may collect certain device-related information such as:
- Device type
- Operating system version
- App usage data

### 3. Payment Information
All payments are processed securely through Google Play Billing. We do not store or process your payment card details.

## How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide and maintain our services
- Display shops and listings in the app
- Process purchases and subscriptions
- Improve the app experience
- Provide customer support

## Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your personal information. We strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services including:

- Google Play Services

These services may collect information used to identify you.

## Children's Privacy

Shopya does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If we discover that a child has provided us personal information, we immediately delete it.
